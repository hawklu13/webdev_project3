Chicago Bulls

My Favorite Players

  • Derrick Rose

    Derrick Rose is my favorite basketball player of all time. He’s very flashy and fun to watch. He always finds a way to get to the rim and lay up the ball. He’s very fast, and I love the fact that he is from Chicago. He’s a hometown hero of sorts. I wish his career didn’t get cut short. He is Chicago’s MVP.

  • Demar Derozan

    Demar was traded to the Bulls in the 2021 season. He surprised everyone with how good of a scorer and how clutch he was. He won us a lot of games with his clutch ability. He got traded not too long ago, but I am happy he played for us and hope he thrives on his new team.

  • Matas Buzelis

    Matas Buzelis is a rookie, just got drafted this past year. He is from the suburbs of Chicago, which I like. He’s a great dunker and plays really good defense. Hope he can show us some more of his potential soon.

My Favorite Game Attended

This game was my favorite player's, Demar's, 1000th game. We got to be really close to the court and were astonished by how beautiful the court looked. I had a great time and hope I can be back soon.
